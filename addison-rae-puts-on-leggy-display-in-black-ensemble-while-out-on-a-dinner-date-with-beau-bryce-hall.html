    Addison Rae is set to star in her first leading role in the film He’s All That, which is a gender-swapped remake of the 1999 teen comedy hit She’s All That.

    Enjoying a break from filming, the TikTok sensation showcased her legs and midriff when she headed out on a romantic dinner date with boyfriend Bryce Hall on Monday.

    The pair were spotted arriving at the seafood restaurant Catch LA, located on the world famous Melrose Ave. in West Hollywood.

    Addison Rae, 20, showed off her fashion sense while out on a romantic dinner date with her boyfriend Bryce Hall in West Hollywood on Monday

    Rae (born Addison Rae Easterling) was first spotted when she stepped out of her white Rolls Royce in style, with all eyes on her toned legs.

    Once on the street, the actress stunned in her all-black ensemble that consisted of a black skirt with a long slit along the left that allowed her to flaunt her toned legs at a moments notice. 

    She also gave a hint of her toned midriff in matching crop top that she wore under a long and shiny black-patterned jacket.

    The Louisiana native rounded out the outfit with black heels, her dark brown tresses pulled back into a bun, and a protective mask over her mouth and nose, in accordance to health and safety protocols related to COVID-19.

    Grand arrival: The TikTok sensation was spotted moments after she parked her car outside the restaurant in the West Hollywood neighborhood of Los Angeles

    Gams for days: The Louisiana native put on a leggy display in a black ensemble when she hopped out of her white Rolls Royce

    The spotlight: Rae and Hall were the center of attention when they made their way to the entrance of the seafood restaurant 

    Hall, 21, opted for casual comfort in black sweatpants, a white hoodie, a black baseball cap and white sneakers.

    The Maryland native, who’s also a prominent TikTok star, also made sure to wear a face mask while out in the public.

    The couple have been romantically linked since October 2019, but they appeared to go their separate ways in the spring 2020, only to reconnect and get back together last June.

    Safety first: The He’s All That star made sure to wear a protective mask over her mouth and nose amid the still raging COVID-19 pandemic

    Chic figure: The skirt had slit on the left, which allowed the TikTok sensation to flaunt her legs at a moments notice

    Transition: The social media star is shooting He’s All That, a gender-swapped remake of the 1999 teen comedy hit She’s All That, which starred Rachael Leigh Cook and Freddie Prinze Jr

    Trending: The social media star has been called the highest-paid personality on TikTok

    Packing on PDA: The two shared a kiss

    Rae joined TikTok in the summer of 2019, and proceeded to upload dance videos to trending songs on her the platform. Within three months she topped over one million followers.

    Since then she has amassed a staggering 75.7 million fans and followers on TikTok, which has earn $5 million in one calendar year ending in June 2020, according to Forbes.

    Her popular dances caught the attention of such celebrities as Kourtney Kardashian, who has since forged a friendship with the budding multi-media star, and landed a role in He’s All That.
