    Valve Corporation, co-founded by Newell in 1996 after he left Microsoft, has been a major player in the gaming industry.     Perhaps the most significant contributor to Newell’s wealth is the digital distribution platform Steam. Launched in 2003, Steam revolutionized the way games are sold and played, providing a centralized platform for purchasing and downloading games.
