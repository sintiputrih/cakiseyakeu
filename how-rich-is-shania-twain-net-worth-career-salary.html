    Shania Twain’s new documentary is planned to premiere on Netflix on July 26, 2022. The film follows her meteoric ascent to fame in the 1990s, her distinct creative vision, and the myriad hurdles she overcame during her long and famous career. Twain has had numerous mainstream songs throughout the years, including You’re Still the One, Any Man of Mine, Man! I’m Feeling Like A Woman From Now On, and That Doesn’t Impress Me Much. Without further ado, continue reading to learn about Shania Twain’s net worth ahead of the impending Netflix documentary.

    Shania Twain has a net worth of roughly $400 million as of April 2023, according to Celebrity Net Worth. Twain, often considered as the Queen of Pop, entered the music business during a period when numerous female pop performers dominated the market. Superstars such as Janet Jackson, Madonna, and Whitney Houston, to name a few.

    Shania Twain’s Net worth

    Twain’s first album, Shania Twain, was a commercial flop. Her sophomore album, The Woman in Me, altered everything. It included multiple big tracks such as You Win My Love, Whose Bed Have Your Boots Been Under? and many more. According to reports, the album has sold an estimated 20 million copies worldwide. Her third album, Come On Over, was another enormous financial success, selling over 40 million copies worldwide. It was also warmly lauded by reviewers and remains on various lists of the best pop albums of all time. Come On Over is one of the best-selling music albums of all time.

    Her albums from the twenty-first century, Up! and Now, were similarly big financial hits while receiving mediocre reviews from current reviewers. She has also headlined a number of tours across the globe over the years. Aside from music, Twain has been in films and television series like I Still Believe, Trading Paint, RuPaul’s Drag Race, and many more. She also allegedly collaborated with the French-American cosmetics company Coty and produced a fragrance named Shania in 2005. Two years later, another fragrance named Shania Starlight was introduced.

    Her revenues from events, films, and sponsorships have also increased her net worth tremendously. Twain remains one of the most popular pop performers of all time, as well as the best-selling female country singer, with over 100 million albums sold.

    A brief glance at Netflix’s documentary Not Just A Girl

    Not Just A Girl delves into Twain’s professional life, her effect on 1990s pop music, and more. According to the official Netflix synopsis:

    “In this documentary, singer Shania Twain spans genres and boundaries as she goes from Nashville novice to worldwide legend.”

    The trailer begins with Twain stating in a voice-over:

    “Taking chances to accomplish things your way might be frightening. You must be courageous.”

    It gives a glimpse into some of her most unforgettable moments. It also features a number of notable artists, including Orville Peck and Lionel Richie, who stated:

    “She was the first to open the door and span musical genres; she was the pathfinder.”

    Several artists in the documentary also discussed her effect as a female performer on the country music landscape. Not Just A Girl will be available on Netflix on Tuesday, July 26, 2022.
