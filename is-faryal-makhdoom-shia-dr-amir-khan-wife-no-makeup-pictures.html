    Is Faryal Makhdoom Shia? Muslim Religion

    Faryal Makhdoom is claimed to belong to the Shia community by The Online Muslim Community Her husband, Amir, however, is a Sunni. The divide between these two groups within the Muslim community is the largest and oldest in history.

    Although both of them follow the same Religion-Islam, their beliefs are different which has divided them for centuries now. Shia believes that just as God alone appoints prophets, God alone has the authority to appoint a prophet’s successor.

    They believe that God chose Ali to be Muhammad’s infallible successor, the first caliph (Khalifah, or head of state) of Islam. Thus, they believed in the lineage of Muhammad’s family through a series of Imams.

    In contrast, Sunni focus on following Prophet’s examples. They believe that human redemption is contingent on faith in Allah and his prophets, acceptance of Muhammad as the final prophet, and belief in righteous deeds as explained in the Koran.

    They believe that Allah’s mercy will determine the redemption of all human beings.

    It is surprising how two people from contradicting beliefs have got married and set up a family. However, it is exemplary that the couple is happily married and have not let their religious belief affect their relationship.
