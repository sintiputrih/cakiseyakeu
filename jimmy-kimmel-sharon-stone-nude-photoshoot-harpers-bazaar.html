    Looks like even Jimmy Kimmel is a fan of Sharon Stone's nude photo shoot for BAZAAR's September issue. The Jimmy Kimmel Live host put the actress in the hot seat during last night's episode, when he brought out a copy of  the issue and pulled up Stone's buzzworthy pictures. 

    "Do you ever look in the mirror and go, 'Wow, thank you, Jesus?" he asked. 

    Though in total awe, Kimmel worried about Stone's children seeing her pictures, but she assured him, "they're not really big Harper's BAZAAR readers." (Yet.)

    "Could I just come on this show all the time?" Stone asked, flattered. Kimmel quipped, "Sure, absolutely! Wear the outfit from the magazine."
