    Poughkeepsie Ice House on the Hudson, Poughkeepsie

    Why It's Outdoor Worthy: The giant patio with its gorgeous river views are just one reason to come here; the other -- aside from the food -- is its proximity to the Walkway over the Hudson. Stop at the restaurant either before or after your walk and relish in this area's gorgeous scenery.

    Enjoy The View: 1 Main St Poughkeepsie, (845) 232-5783, www.poughkeepsieicehouse.com/.
