    Ralph Ineson Biography

    Ralph Ineson born as Ralph Michael Ineson is an English actor, best known as Dagmer Cleftjaw in Game of Thrones, Amycus Carrow in the last three Harry Potter films and Chris Finch in the British Broadcasting Corporation series The Office.

    In the 2012-2013 British Broadcasting Corporation Radio 4 dramatizations of the ten Martin Beck police novels by Sjöwall and Wahlöö, Ineson played policeman Gunvald Larsson. television adverts that have used his voice include lastminute.com, Dacia cars, Gaviscon and Wickes.

    Ralph Ineson Age

    Ineson was born on 15 December 1969 in Leeds, United Kingdom. He is 49 years old as of 2019.

    Ralph Ineson Height

    He stands at a height of 6 feet 2 inches taller.

    Ralph Ineson Family|Education

    Ralph Ineson was born Ralph Michael Ineson on the 15th of December, 1969 in Leeds, West Riding of Yorkshire, England and he was born to his father Michael Ineson and to his mother Bumble Ineson.

    Ralph Ineson attended Woodleigh School, located in North Yorkshire and Pocklington School; he later studied at Furness College, Lancaster University, where he graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree.

    Ralph Ineson Game Of Thrones

    In 2015, he starred alongside his fellow Game of Thrones cast member Kate Dickie in the critically acclaimed horror film The Witch.
