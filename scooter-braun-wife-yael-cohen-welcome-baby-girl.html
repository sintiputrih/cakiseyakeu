    Scooter Braun, Wife Yael Cohen Welcome Baby Girl

    And baby makes five! Scooter Braun and wife,Yael Cohen, are now the proud parents of a daughter,Hart Violet Braun. The 32-year-old gave birth to the couples first girl on Saturday, December 1, eight days after she was due.

    09 Sep 2024, 00:00

    And baby makes five! Scooter Braun and wife, Yael Cohen, are now the proud parents of a daughter, Hart Violet Braun.

    The 32-year-old gave birth to the couple’s first girl on Saturday, December 1, eight days after she was due.

    “She came on her own schedule and her own time. Saturday morning (8 days late) we became 5…. and we wouldn’t want it any other way,” Justin Bieber and Ariana Grande’s manager, 37, wrote on Instagram along with a picture of his wife touching skin-to-skin with the newborn on Monday, December 3. “Welcome to the party Hart Violet Braun! Daddy will always be here! And mommy… Mommy is a rockstar!! We love you! #sheownsme”

    Cohen shared the same photo on her own Instagram page and simply wrote, “We’re so in love. Hart Violet Braun,” along with a purple heart.

    The talent manager announced the exciting pregnancy news on his 37th birthday on June 18.

    “But the best part of this birthday is I got my wish… OUR wish… #anotherone @yael.” he captioned an Instagram pic of his family as he lovingly rested his hand on Cohen’s baby bump.

    Cohen, the founder and CEO of F—ck Cancer, gushed about her third pregnancy on Instagram, as well, posting a picture of herself wearing a bikini with her hands making a heart over her growing belly.

    “Happy Birthday my love, let’s have another baby to celebrate,” she captioned the sweet photo.
