    Smokie Norful’s Successful Career in Gospel Music

    Smokie Norful, an American gospel singer and pianist, has a net worth of $3 million. Born in October 1975, Norful earned a master’s of divinity and worked as a history teacher and an educational director. He was ordained an elder in the African Methodist Episcopal church and has served as a minister of music, youth pastor, and associate minister. Currently, Norful is the pastor of the Victory Cathedral Worship Center in Bolingbrook, Illinois.

    Norful’s music career started with his debut studio album, I Need You Now, which was released in 2002. His second studio album, Nothing Without You, won a Grammy Award for Best Contemporary Soul Gospel Album in 2004. In 2015, Norful won another Grammy Award for his song “No Greater Love”. He has also won four Dove Awards and three Gospel Music Excellence Awards and has been nominated for an NAACP Image Award.

    In addition to his successful career in gospel music, Smokie and his wife Carla own and operate four businesses.
