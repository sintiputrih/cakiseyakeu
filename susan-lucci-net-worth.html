    Susan Lucci Wiki Biography

    On 23 December 1946, Susan Victoria Lucci was born in Scarsdale, New York State USA, to an Italian mother and a father of French, Swedish, and German descent. Lucci has experienced a successful career in the entertainment industry and is best known for her role as Erica Kane in the ABC daytime drama “All My Children”. Her estimated net worth is $45 million, as of mid-2016, with much of her wealth attributed to her entertainment career.

    Susan Lucci Net Worth $45 Million

    Lucci pursued her education at Garden City High School in New York and earned a bachelor’s degree in drama from Marymount College. Her portrayal of Erica in “All My Children” propelled her to fame, earning her numerous award nominations. Lucci’s success streak was interrupted in 1999 when she finally won her first Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama Series award, after being nominated almost every consecutive year since 1978.

    In addition to her iconic role in “All My Children”, Lucci has appeared in various television shows and films including “Young Doctors in Love”, “Anastasia: The Mystery of Anna”, “Ebbie”, and “Devious Maids”. She has also hosted the documentary television series “Deadly Affairs” and participated in the seventh season of “Dancing with the Stars” in 2008.

    Lucci’s accolades include People’s Choice Awards, Soap Opera Digest Awards, and the Ride of Fame Award, reflecting her significant impact on the entertainment industry.

    Outside of her professional endeavors, Susan Lucci has been married to Helmut Huber since 1969 and they have a daughter and a son.
