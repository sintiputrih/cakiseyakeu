    In a short period of time, Sydney Sweeney has really blown up in popularity. Despite her newfound fame and success, though, Sydney's still grounded in reality. The Euphoria star will still take roles just to make money. As she becomes richer, she'll probably become pickier with her roles, but for now, that's how she continues to do things.

    Related: Euphoria Stars Reveal What They Want To See In Season Three

    As she becomes more famous, she will become more different than the average person. She's already said that her fans, and even her family, don't understand her. This is due to her A-list status, something those groups of people don't have in common with her. She still is a person at the end of the day. She is unique and has her own preferences. For instance, Sydney doesn't drink coffee, opting for water and candy instead.

    Something Sydney does have in common with fans is that she can be quite obsessed with some things. Most girls her age can be quite obsessive over various celebrities, and from that perspective, she truly is no different.

    9 Sydney Sweeney's Obsession With Zendaya

    Sydney is straight-up obsessed with her A-list co-star. They're best friends in real life and have translated that friendship to social media. Sydney's even cried for Zendaya's historic Emmy win, admitting to it many times.

    Related: Tom Holland's Real Feelings About Euphoria And Zendaya's Heartbreaking Performance

    The duo has shown up to red-carpet events with each other to represent Euphoria. They don't always talk about each other, but everyone knows they love each other.

    8 Everything To Know About Sydney Sweeney's Obsession With Maude Apatow

    While Sydney is tight with Zendaya, her biggest friendship appears to be with another Euphoria co-star, Maude Apatow.

    Once again, they post about each other on social media and talk about each other in interviews. Sydney proclaimed to People that Maude was her ride or die, and Maude responded by calling Sydney an "unstoppable force".

    To top it all off, Sydney and Maude have called each other "sis" on social media. That's how you know a friendship is really serious.

    7 What Sydney Sweeney Has Said About Alexa Demie

    One more Euphoria co-star is Alexa Demie. She plays Maddie Perez on the show. Yes, Sydney also happens to be obsessed with her, too.

    The two attend red-carpet events with each other and take joint selfies. Most importantly, Sydney attended an Oscars after-party with Alexa, and the two were pictured having the time of their lives. In an interview with People, Sydney said that Alexa "rocks" out as an actor.

    A funny fact about their friendship is that their characters hate each other on Euphoria. The two characters fought each other and were even nominated for "Best Fight" at the MTV Awards. Alexa actually broke Sydney's toe during that famous fight.

    6 Sydney Sweeney Thinks Highly Of Alexandra Daddario

    Sydney has knocked it out of the park as Olivia in The White Lotus. The main star on that show is Alexandra Daddario, someone Sydney has really looked up to as an actress.

    At the GQ Men Of The Year event in 2022, Alexandra said Sydney is the "perfect package". They're pictured together all the time, and recently re-created a scene from The White Lotus for Instagram. Both of these actresses have a lot in common, such as appearing in shows and movies for teenagers. Alexandra starred in Texas Chainsaw 3D. She once claimed the house they filmed in was definitely "haunted".

    5 Sydney Sweeney's Obsession With The Property Brothers

    It's expected that Sydney would love her co-stars, but she's also completely into a pair of reality stars.

    She's admitted to being obsessed with the Property Brothers, Jonathan and Drew Scott. For those who don't know them, they're brothers who create and renovate homes on their personal reality show. Sydney admitted to "fangirling" over them when she met them at an exclusive celebrity event.

    During the YouTube interview where she admitted to this, she gave an explanation as to why she was a fan of the Property Brothers. She said she grew up renovating and remodeling houses with her parents, and now that's making money she's doing it on her own.

    4 Sydney Sweeney Thinks Dakota Johnson Is Hot...

    Another celebrity Sydney has fangirled over is Dakota Johnson. Yes, they're also going to be co-stars in the upcoming Madame Web movie. Before that, though, Sydney was obsessed with Dakota due to her roles in the 50 Shades of Gray movies.

    Sydney said in an interview with Variety that she secretly watched those films as a teenager. When she met Dakota, she was nervous and intimated by her. Sydney even called Dakota "hot".

    3 However, Sydney Sweeney's Real Childhood Crush Is Sonic The Hedgehog

    Sydney's real childhood crush was truly Sonic the Hedgehog, though. She stated in an interview with MTV she was in love with the video game character as a kid, in an odd but romantic way.

    Aside from a Call of Duty ad cameo, Sydney has never really appeared to be interested in gaming overall. There is certainly a love and obsession for an iconic furry character, though.

    2 Sydney Sweeney Adores Alexander McQueen

    If you know Sydney at all, there's one thing she's undoubtedly in love with. She is 100% obsessed with fashion.

    Related: Euphoria Changed Sydney Sweeney's Relationship With Instagram And Social Media Forever

    In particular, Sydney seems to be obsessed with Alexander McQueen. Recently, Sydney showed up to a London event dressed head to toe in an Alexander McQueen outfit. This isn't even the first time in the past year she wore the luxurious fashion brand to a red carpet event, as she wore an all-black Alexander McQueen suit jacket to a private event in Los Angeles. There's no doubt that she is utterly captivated by everything the designer puts out.

    1 Brigitte Bardot Is Sydney Sweeney's Biggest Fashion And Beauty Role Model

    Sydney has admitted that, although she loves and rocks many different fashion brands, her biggest influence is an icon who's been around long before Sydney was born. She proclaimed this in an interview with Coveteur. In fact, Sydney said that she had been looking up to Brigitte Bardot since she was around 16 years old.

    For those who are unaware, Brigitte was one of the original beauty icons from the 1960s. She influenced pop culture with her appearances in hit movies such as Contempt and Don Juan. She was also a world-famous model, and if you look at some of her photo shoots you can definitely see some of the same poses and expressions Sydney uses in her modeling pictures.
