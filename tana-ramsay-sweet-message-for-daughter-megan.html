    Gordon Ramsay’s wife Tana’s words were sure to have given 24-year-old Megan a big boost

      This past weekend was a big one for Tana Ramsay’s daughter Megan - and her mum was there supporting her all the way.  The 24-year-old daughter of Tana and her husband Gordon Ramsay was running the London Marathon on Sunday, April 23, and proud mum Tana helped her prepare.

      Tana did Meg’s hair in plaits ahead of the 26.2-mile race, which Meg ran to raise funds for Great Ormand Street Hospital. And the preparation session clearly brought back memories of times gone by.

      Posting a sweet good luck message on Instagram along with a picture of Meg’s perfectly-done hair, Tana wrote: “Doing Meg’s hair in French plaits reminded me of getting her ready for school all those years ago… Best of luck Meg for @tcslondonmarathon running for @greaterormondst xxxx” 

      Tana signed off my tagging her husband Gordon into the sweet post, which was flooded with messages of support from friends and followers. “Good luck Meg, amazing,” wrote Jamie Oliver’s wife Jools along with an explosion and heart emoji.
