    Flavor Flav's Chicken &amp; Ribs Hit With Eviction Notice

    Famed Public Enemy hype man Flavor Flavs hopes of owning a successful chain of fast food restaurants, fittingly titled Flavor Flavs Chicken &amp; Ribs, are beginning to crumble with the last remaining restaurant now facing eviction.

    25 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Famed Public Enemy hype man Flavor Flav’s hopes of owning a successful chain of fast food restaurants, fittingly titled Flavor Flav’s Chicken & Ribs, are beginning to crumble with the last remaining restaurant now facing eviction.

    TMZ reports that the former reality star was recently handed eviction papers for the Flavor Flav’s Chicken & Ribs location in Sterling Heights, Michigan. But according to Flav, who reportedly owes his landlord $25,000, he was unaware of his restaurant’s rent situation.

    “I’m gonna figure out what’s going on and get to the bottom of this ASAP,” he revealed to TMZ.

    AD LOADING...

    Court documents in the matter show that the locations $3,500 monthly rent payment was late in both September and October and was not received in December and the months following.

    Previous Flavor Flav’s Chicken & Ribs locations in Iowa and Nevada were closed in 2011 and 2012. It’s unclear when exactly the restaurant will face eviction, although the landlord is hoping for an immediate move.
