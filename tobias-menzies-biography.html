    Who is Tobias Menzies?

    Tobias Menzies is an English television, stage, and film actor.

    He is best known for his role as Edmure Tully in HBO’s Game of Thrones and Brutus in HBO’s ‘Rome’.

    Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

    Tobias Menzies was born on 7 March 1974 in London, England. Tobias was born Hanan Tobias Simpson Menzies.

    Tobias Menzies was born to his parents Gillian (née Simpson), English literature and drama teacher, and Peter Menzies, a BBC radio producer.

    He has a brother named Luke Menzies, who is now a solicitor.

    Tobias holds British citizenship but his ethnicity is English-Scottish.

    Education, School/College University

    Tobias Menzies attended the Perry Court Rudolf Steiner School and trained in movement, singing, and instrumental music which is collectively known as the Steiner System.

    After that, he joined Frensham Heights School and then attended Deborah Moody’s Year Out Drama Company in Stratford-Upon-Avon.

    Then, he joined the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art and graduated with a BA degree in Acting in 1998.

    Tobias Menzies: Professional Life and Career

    Tobias Menzies worked with a British improv comedy company called The Spontaneity Shop.

    After that, he started his acting career with the series Casualty’ in 1998.

    Then, he appeared in the TV film ‘Summer in the Suburbs’, ‘Midsomer Murders’, ‘Ultimate Force’, and many more.

    He made his film debut in the 2000’s dramatic comedy ‘The Low Down’ which premiered at the Locarno Film Festival.

    In 2005, he appeared in the romantic comedy ‘Piccadilly Jim and in the drama ‘Pierrepoint: The Last Hangman’.

    Moreover, Tobias Menzies has appeared in ‘Rome’ (2005–07), ‘Casino Royale’ (2006), ‘Spooks’, as Edmure Tully in ‘Game of Thrones, ‘The Thick of It’,  ‘The Honourable Woman’ (2014), ‘Outlander’ (2014), ‘The Terror’ (2016), ‘The Crown’ (2018).

    Tobias Menzies is also active in theaters and has appeared in ‘The History Boys, ‘Three Sisters’, ‘Hamlet’,  ‘Persuasion’  (2007), ‘The Cherry Orchard, ‘The Relief of Belsen’ (2007).

    Likewise, he started his radio career with 2010’s drama ‘A Nice Little Holiday’.

    Also, he starred in BBC Radio 3’s drama ‘Serious Money’ and narrated RISINGTIDEFALLINGSTAR in 2017.

    Awards, Nominations

    Tobias Menzies was nominated for Golden Globe Award for the Best Performance by an Actor in a Supporting Role in a Series ‘Outlander’ in 2016.

    Also, he was nominated for Saturn Award in the category Best Actor on Television for ‘Outlander’ in 2015.
