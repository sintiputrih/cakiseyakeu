    What is the best thing to pawn for $50?

    Items that are often pawned for around $50 would include some electronics like a used laptop or high-quality headphones. Designer belts or sunglasses might also be valued around $50.

    What pawn shops pay most for?

    Generally speaking, pawn shops tend to pay the most for items such as gemstones, electronics, firearms (registered to you), power tools and equipment, antiques/collectibles, musical instruments, sporting goods, and rare coins.

    What sells for $100 at a pawn shop?

    Is it better to pawn or sell?

    Whether to pawn or sell is a personal choice. If you want to get your items back, pawning is the best option. However, if you are looking to get rid of your items permanently, selling would be the better choice.
