    Let’s find out more about the protester, who has been creating buzz.

    There is no general consensus about the act of Barney; some people say that she is brave to take extreme steps, and other things she is just too much to watch and is not the image people want to associate with the pro-choice movement.

    The United States has been swayed by protestors after a document about the reversal of Roe v Wade was leaked. People on the right are quite happy to see that this decision is acting now, but the left is completely devasted by this.

    Who Is Crackhead Barney?

    Crackhead is a performer and ambush interviewer. She can often be seen asking people questions about various current trends. She seems to have a penchant for left ideology and can be seen in left leaded protests.

    However, she appears to be a person who does not follow everything about any particular side. She has expressed her disagreement with Joe Biden and Kamala Harris. She has claimed that the president and vice-president have done unfair treatment of African- Americans.

    She is more of a social media sensation, who can be seen dressed up completely in an unconventional manner and walking around asking questions. Her videos often go viral.

    Anti Abortion Protest

    Since the leak of documents indicating that the supreme court might reverse Roe v Wade, the US has seen a great number of protests across the country about anti-abortion. People are expressing their strong disapproval.

    However, not people are angry that people on right seem to be very happy with this indication of reversal of the case about abortion law. If Roe v Wade is reversed, the abortion rule will go to the state.

    By this, it means that the state can make its own rules about abortion. People are infuriated by this decision; some of the prominent figures from the democratic party have shown their strong opposition to it.

    Crackhead Barney Age & Husband

    There is no information about her husband and her partner. She lives actually a very private life, not sharing her personal information with the public.
