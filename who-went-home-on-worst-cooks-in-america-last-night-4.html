    The American reality television show Worst Cooks in America is a culinary competition that airs on Food Networks as well as Discovery Plus.

    Worst Cooks in America Love at First Bite has 16 single contestants who are referred to as recruits through a culinary boot camp and have poor cooking skills.

    Later, the recruits of the cooking competition show are trained in the various cooking techniques that include baking, knife skills, temperature, seasoning, and preparation.

    And finally, whoever succeeds in cooking a restaurant-quality, three-course meal for three food critics wins the show and takes $25,000 cash amount.

    This cooking show is successfully running for 26 seasons, and the latest season of the Food Network show premiered on August 6, 2023.

    Who Went Home On Worst Cooks In America Last Night?

    Louisa Tocquie, Allegra Melton, Etherio Noon and Sterlin Quinn went home in Worst Cooks in America last night. The episode aired on 10th September.

    The seventh episode of The Worst Cooks in America was named Love At First Bite What Happens in Boot Camp. It is available to watch on Food Network and HBO Max.

    The show’s premise was that two mentors, and also judges, and their recruits use clues to retrace their steps from the previous night and locate a misplaced dish.

    All of them had been partying the night before, and after successfully finding the dish, the recruits learned about plating and presentation, creating a showstopping fish dish for the judges to evaluate.

    The latest episode of the cooking competition was a blast. The contestants worked in pairs, putting in their best efforts to create a winning dish. The stakes were high since two couples were to be eliminated before the finale.

    The semi-final episode of Worst Cooks in America was a hit among the fans, who expressed their enjoyment on Twitter, labeling it as one of the funniest shows to binge-watch.

    One Twitter used tweeted,” If you ever want to feel better about your cooking skills, watch Worst Cooks in America” Another added,” I can rewatch Worst Cooks in America over and over again.”

    The finale episode of the Food Network cooking competition show will air on September 11, 2023. Sami White and Jessica Singer will compete to be the title winner.

    Where Is Worst Cooks In America Filmed?

    Worst Cooks in America is filmed in New York City, New York on Food Network’s own studio-based production.

    The production company studio is located at Chelsea Market in New York, Manhattan. There are many shows that are filmed in this studio.

    According to The Cinemaholic, Chelsea Market is an outstanding location to shoot the cooking show as the market is a center point for foodies.

    Chelsea Market offers visitors a variety of delicious eating options and several fun activities that can be enjoyed by every one of them.

    There is a food hall, several exotic shopping malls, office buildings, and TV production facilities available in that particular location.

    This Chelsea Market is a well-known and admired tourist attraction that is exactly located at 75 Ninth Avenue, between 15th and 16th Street, Manhattan.

    However, Food Network’s staple show Worst Cooks in America season 17 was filmed in GUM Studios in Long Island City.
