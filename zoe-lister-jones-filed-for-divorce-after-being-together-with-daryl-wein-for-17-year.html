    After 17 years of marriage, Zoe Lister-Jones is divorcing her husband, Daryl Wein. She recently divorced Daryl, citing irreconcilable differences in the court documents.

    In 2021, the actress revealed that she had already divorced Wein, with the divorce date set for February 1, 2021. Zoe does not want the court to award spousal support because the couple has no children. She has also asked the court to keep things simple when it comes to asset distribution and to uphold the formal separation decision issued in May 2021.

    New Girl’s Zoe Lister-Jones character

    Zoe Lister-Jones played Fawn Moscato in the Fox comedy New Girl. Moscato is a Los Angeles city councilwoman who has appeared as a recurring character in five episodes of the show. The protagonist is a tough and intimidating politician. While dating Schmidt, she attempted to manage him and turn him into a political asset, and it worked.

    Moscato had Schmidt take the fall after her image of reaching down to pick up a golf ball while wearing no pants went viral. Fawn and Schmidt split up after the former asked Schmidt to marry her so that the media could focus solely on them. New Girl premiered in September 2011 and ended in May 2018. The show aired for seven seasons and 146 episodes, and it received rave reviews from critics and fans alike.

    Daryl Wein and Zoe Lister-Jones’ relationship

    Zoe-Lister Jones and Daryl Wein married in 2013, despite the fact that it is unknown how they met. They even collaborated on a few projects, such as Lola Versus, Consumed, and How It Ends. She defined herself as being in and out of an open relationship in an episode of the WTF with Marc Maron podcast discussing her marriage. Despite the fact that she and Wein had agreed on the arrangement, she claimed that Zoe was unhappy because Daryl was seeing other people. She stated, ”

    “I will say that when your lover returns home after f***ing someone else, it is a difficult day.” I believe changing the programming will be difficult… It’s difficult not to feel envious or possessive.”

    Zoe Lister-Jones became well-known for her role in the CBS comedy Life in Pieces. She has appeared in other shows such as Delocated, Whitney, and New Girl, and she made her directorial debut with Band-Aid in 2017.

    In contrast, Daryl Wein is a well-known director, producer, and actor who has directed films such as Unlocked, Lola Versus, Consumed, and others. He appeared in a number of films, including Magic Rock, The Hebrew Hammer, Ed, and others.
